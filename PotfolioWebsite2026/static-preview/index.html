Words of Wisdom

A few designer truths from the tide pool

Good design should feel easy for the user and slightly haunted for the designer.

If the layout looks effortless, please know there were at least fourteen tiny spacing crises behind it.

My favorite feedback is "this just makes sense" because that usually means the hard part is invisible.
